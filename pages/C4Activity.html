Our Signature Dish: the Pina Rellena.
This dish is as delicious as it is visually stunning. Featuring several savory seafoods, the gooey cheese melted on top is simply the first layer to this complex meal. First, the pineapple is sliced in half and the pineapple meat is carved out. It is then diced finely and combined with cream, butter, and gouda cheese. Mussels, clams, shrimp, octopus, and scallops are all incorporated into the mixture, poured back into the hollowed out pineapple, and covered in another layer of cheese to be broiled.
Perfect for sharing!
